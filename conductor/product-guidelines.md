# Product Guidelines: rust-yscraper

## Prose Style
- **Minimalist & Brief**: Keep documentation and user-facing text brief and focused only on essential details. Avoid conversational filler.
- **Direct**: Use clear, concise language to communicate functionality and status.

## UX Principles
- **Material Design**: Follow Material Design principles for a modern, clean, and consistent user interface.
- **Utility and Clarity**: Ensure the interface highlights the most important information (e.g., scrape status, new comments) without unnecessary clutter.

## Quality Standards
- **High Test Coverage**: Maintain a high standard for test coverage (aiming for >80%) for all new features and bug fixes.
- **Robust Error Handling**: Ensure all user inputs are validated and errors (especially in the scraper) are handled gracefully and logged clearly.
- **Documentation & Best Practices**: Code should be thoroughly documented (sparingly, focusing on 'why') and follow the project's established conventions.

## Visual Style
- **Neutral & Clean**: A neutral, clean visual style that focuses on content and data. Use a professional and modern developer tool aesthetic that doesn't distract from the information being aggregated.
